# Dark & Light Mode

## What We Added

We added a **theme toggle button** to the top-right corner of the app. It looks like a 🌙 moon (in light mode) or a ☀️ sun (in dark mode). Clicking it instantly switches the entire app between a light and dark color scheme.

The app also **remembers your preference** — if you switch to dark mode and come back later, it stays in dark mode. It will even automatically match your computer's system setting the first time you open it.

## Why Dark Mode Is Useful

**Easier on the eyes** — Bright white screens emit a lot of light. In a dim room or at night, staring at a bright screen can cause eye strain, headaches, and fatigue. Dark mode reduces the amount of light coming from the screen, making it much more comfortable for long working sessions.

**Better for focus** — Many developers and designers prefer dark mode because the content stands out more clearly against a dark background, reducing visual noise.

**Saves battery** — On laptops and phones with OLED or AMOLED screens, dark mode can meaningfully extend battery life since dark pixels use less power than bright ones.

**Personal preference** — Some people simply find it more aesthetically pleasing, especially for creative tools like Component Studio where you're staring at the screen for extended periods.

## What It Looks Like

| Light Mode | Dark Mode |
|---|---|
| Warm off-white background | Deep warm charcoal background |
| Dark text for readability | Light warm text for readability |
| Moon 🌙 icon in header | Sun ☀️ icon in header |

Both themes maintain the same warm, calm color palette — dark mode isn't harsh or stark, it's designed to feel just as comfortable as the light version.
